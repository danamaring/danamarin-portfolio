Web Designer & Front-end Developer

Thermosecurity Project - The situation

THE SITUATION

The challenge was to create a website in 24 hours that was fully functional and that presented the products of "Thermosecurity" a fictitious company that sells devices to control different features of a house (temperature, alarm, security cameras).

Thermosecurity Project - The solution

THE SOLUTION

We worked in teams. My role was to design and build the front-end part of the website considering our target (30+ years old adults). The result was a fully responsive website with a clean design that presented the main features of the app. We did extensive research on the different already-existing companies that sell similar products to make sure that the website made sense from the UX/UI perspective.

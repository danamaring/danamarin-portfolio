eCigarrete Project - The situation

THE SITUATION

The project’s instruction was to design 3 printed ads for the Canadian Government campaign whose goal is to raise awareness among teenagers about the danger of using e-cigarettes.

eCigarrete Project - The solution

THE SOLUTION

The result is the next three magazine ads that were created thinking of the target audience: teenagers. Some of the main points that were considered for this ads are that teenagers don’t like to be told what to do or what not to do, so instead of creating a big ad saying ‘DO NOT USE E-CIGARETTES’ I decided to use the most popular flavours to begin the sentence with that instead of giving an order, leaves a feeling of curiosity in the user that will direct them to continue reading the ad and, therefore, educating themselves on the topic.
